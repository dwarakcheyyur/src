About Us How it all began

What Makes Us Tick!

CloudReign Technologies started in 2012, helping many Businesses and Educational institutions to leverage the benefits of Cloud Computing. A full service provider of messaging, productivity and collaboration suites. CloudReign helps organizations of every kind and size, at every stage: from planning the optimal architecture for your transition to the cloud, to making the most of Google, and/or Zoho unique enterprise cloud offerings.

CloudReign is a Google partner, authorized to service G Suite and Chrome, with an expert team transforming businesses with cloud services. Successful in many large G Suite migrations, adoption drive, change management, complex implementations & workflows, from laptops to large fleet of Chromebooks with minimum fuss, loss of time improving productivity. We strategise and enable cloud worker in enterprises. We do digital classroom as a service transforming to 21st century school.

CloudReign is also a Zoho workplace partner, authorized to service Zoho workplace. We have a team of experienced professionals who can handle email solutions of any size. We are capable of implementing / migrating mail for your organization without disrupting your day to day activities.

CloudReign has extensive knowledge in building Chrome applications and extending G Suite applications with Google Apps Script, lightweight application development in the G Suite platform. CloudReign has done more than 240 change management and transformation training backed by a team of experienced Google certified professionals, bringing years of expertise in G Suite & Chrome.

Our Mission

To provide customers with accurate product knowledge and full service that allow them to concentrate on their core business rather than managing non-core issues like messaging collaboration etc. Our friendly, knowledgeable and professional staff will help inspire, educate and solve problem for our customers.
